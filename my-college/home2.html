Introduction:

NIT Warangal is an institute of national importance in India, situated in Warangal, Telangana. It is the oldest NIT established in 1959 as REC and renamed as NIT in 2002. It holds its position at 19th in NIRF ranking and 4th among other NITs. It has a magnificent campus spread over an area of 256 acres. It provides education in the field of science and technology. It offers 8 UG(B.Tech) and 25 PG(M.Tech) programs. It provides ample facilities and modern technologies which gives a new height to the learning experience.

Innovation & Learning :

NIT Warangal is equipped with cutting-edge technology to offer a great learning experience. Classrooms are well-planned and have all kinds of accessories like projector, smart board and what not. This college has specialized labs for research and development works. In recent times, from NIT Warangal over 20 patents has been published and 3 has been granted. NIT Warangal has received over 5cr as sponsored research fund and over 2.6cr as consultancy research fund. NITW also provides a centre for innovation and incubation to promote innovations leading to new processes, products, designs and technologies in collaboration with industry and thereby facilitate application of knowledge to the society.

Teacher-Student relation:

NITW offers a healthy teacher-student relationship. Faculties here are extremely passionate about teaching and guide their students in all sorts of manners. Students are taught according to the latest trend to become industry-ready. Now the total faculty strength at NIT Warangal stands at 342, which is the highest among all 31 NIT's across India. Apart from this nearly 269 Ph.D., (150 Full Time and 119 Part time) Scholars are there to assist professors in labs and project works to gain valuable experience. Last year this institute has taken an initiative where each faculty has been assigned to a small group of students as a mentor. This is indeed an appreciable work from the authority because, now students not only can take advice from faculty regarding studies but also can discuss their other problems with them.

Ragging free campus:

Ragging is strictly prohibited in the campus. Any kind of activity of ragging is not tolerated and strict disciplinary action is taken against them. Rather, seniors here are quite friendly and helpful. In fact their experiences in the campus interviews help juniors in terms of placement preparations.

Placements:

Placement is one of the things, almost every student looks forward to it since they took admission. The Institute's Centre for Career Planning and Development (CCPD) provides a launch pad for students to make it to the best organizations in the fields of software, core industries, management, etc. The biggest names in the corporate world visit the campus on a regular basis, with enviable recruitment offers. The CCPD boasts of maintaining an excellent rapport with a wide spectrum of companies spread across various industry sectors. Over the years, the section has maintained good and strong relationship with Industries across the country and as a result, has built up an impressive placement record both in terms of percentage of registered students placed, as well as the number of companies visiting the campus. About 150 - 180 companies visit the campus every year.
